# Addons

## Actions

[Actions documentation](https://github.com/storybookjs/storybook/tree/master/addons/actions)

Go to [Checkbox - checked](/?path=/story/components-checkbox--checked) and [Date Picker - Primary](/?path=/story
/components-date-picker--primary) and open the **Actions tab**. Then interact with the component and look at what is going on in the tab.

<img src={actionsTab} alt="Actions tab" />

## Knobs

[Knobs documentation](https://github.com/storybookjs/storybook/tree/master/addons/knobs)

Take a look at the **Knobs tab** in [Button](/?path=/story/components-button--defaut) and [Accordion](/?path=/story/components-accordion--defaut).
This addon allows you to change the props provided to the component dynamically.

<img src={knobsTab} alt="Knobs tab" />

## Accessibility

[Accessibility documentation](https://github.com/storybookjs/storybook/tree/master/addons/a11y)

Take a look at [ContentSwitcher](/?path=/story/components-contentswitcher--defaut), you can see that the addon A11y detects 1 violation.

<img
  src={a11yTab}
  alt="The a11y addon detects 1 violation in the ContentSwitcher"
/>

## Viewport

[Viewport documentation](https://github.com/storybookjs/storybook/tree/master/addons/viewport)

Take a look at [Accordion](/?path=/story/components-accordion--defaut), click on the **viewport icon** and change the viewport size and the orientation.

<img
  src={vewportIcon}
  alt="You can find the viewport icon in the Sandbox toolbar"
/>

## Docs

[Docs documentation](https://github.com/storybookjs/storybook/tree/master/addons/docs)

You can already see the current page which has been created thanks to this addon.
You can also take a look at the [Date Picker](/?path=/docs/components-date-picker--primary) and click on the **Docs tab** to see the generated doc.

<img src={docsTab} alt="The DocsPage is automatically generated" />
